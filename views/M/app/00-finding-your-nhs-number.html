Everyone registered with the NHS has a unique patient identifier called an NHS Number.

An NHS number is a 10 digit number, like 485 777 3456.

You should be able to find your NHS Number on any letter or document you have received from the NHS. This includes:

  • prescriptions
  • test results
  • hospital referral or appointment letters

If you have a medical card, your NHS number should be printed on it.

If you cannot find your NHS number, there are other ways to set your preferences instead
