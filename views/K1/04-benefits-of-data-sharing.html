Research

Prevent serious illness

Develop new treatments

Make quicker diagnoses

Your patient health information provides numerous benefits. It is used in research to find cures and better treatments for diseases like diabetes and cancer.

Without this data, the NHS are unable to develop or improve health and care services for the future.

Planning

Plan NHS health services

Reduce length of stay

Improve individual care

Patient health information can also be used to manage and prepare health and social care services. With this information the NHS can plan where to place essential services, like heart units and specialist care wards.

Knowledge

Learn more about diseases

Evaluate NHS policy

Improve patient safety

With all of this information, the NHS can learn more about illnesses and develop our understanding. This helps to improve health and care services for you and your family.
