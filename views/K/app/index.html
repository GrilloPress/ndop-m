How to register your preference online

For security reasons we must verify who you are before you can set your preference. We will do this by searching for your contact details in our database.

1

Enter your details

Tell us your name, date of birth and NHS number. We can then find your contact information in our patient database.

2

Receive a code

Using the contact details we hold in our database, we will send you a one-time verification code.

3

Set your preference

When your code is verified, you will be able to set your preference online.

You agree to let us search for your details in our database, so that we can send you a one-time security passcode.
